WHAT IS BACKSPACE?

Today’s social networks are cluttered with old posts, pictures you might not want to see, and videos that aren’t relevant to your interests. Not to mention, you see tons of re-posted articles and videos. What ever happened to real people sharing their real experiences and thoughts with their friends as they happen? Without this, the whole social networking experience is ruined for all of us! Social networking is supposed to be fun, personal, fresh, and real! Now, it can be once again! Introducing Backspace!

Express yourself with photos, short videos, and status updates. Don’t worry about worry about your posts lasting forever on Backspace. Everything shared on Backspace disappears in 24 hours. Here’s the twist: The more popular your photos, videos, and status updates, the longer they stay around. Interaction from your friends keep your posts alive for longer so more of your friends have a chance to see it!

We believe that nothing you do on a social network should last forever. An in-person conversation with your friend doesn’t last forever and neither does anything on Backspace.

We're excited to change the future of social networking! With your help, we can make backspace the new place created for YOU where everyone goes to finally socialize freely!

    • NOTHING ON BACKSPACE LASTS FOREVER
